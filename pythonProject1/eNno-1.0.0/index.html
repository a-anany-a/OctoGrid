About Us

About Us

Enhance the accessibility and affordability of cooling solutions, addressing the rising issue of heat stress in tropical climates.

OctoGrid offers a unique AI-powered, plug-and-play device – OctoCool

  • that can be retrofitted and optimizes air conditioner usage to reduce energy costs.
  • leverages predictive analytics to adjust energy consumption.
  • contributes to a more sustainable and resilient electric grid in Singapore.

OctoGrid has the potential to create lasting social and environmental impact by making smart cooling solutions accessible to everyone in Singapore, reducing household electricity bills, and lowering carbon emissions.
